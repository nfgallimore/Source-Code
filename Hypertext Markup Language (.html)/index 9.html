About

Welcome to my page!

This is the official website of Nicholas F. Gallimore.

I'm twenty years young from Akron, Ohio.

I completely understand if you've never heard of Akron, so feel free to ask me if you REALLY want to know what Ohio is like.

I've been involved in the computer world for a while, not so much the programming side but when it comes to networks and unix systems I consider 127.0.0.1 and the command line my home away from home.

At 19 I became the founder and self proclaimed CEO of a small computer company that offers a variety of computer solutions, services, and consulting to a variety of clients ranging from everyday consumers to local businesses.

When I say I can fix any computer problem, I back it up with my never ending search for even the most difficult of problems which are constantly being deemed "unfixable" by competitiors.

With that being said, I've serviced tens of thousands of systems varying from ATM's, bank teller machines, and even antique Slot Machines.

In addition to iMac's, PC's, and normal laptops, I've organized, deployed, built, and installed even the "biggest" of the enterprise servers. Ranging in quantities of a thousand plus at a time, I literally have done it so much I wake up in the morning sometimes muttering out "grep that puppet!". Okay, yeah so only once in a great while.

Would I say I'm good with computers? Humble, but yes. Computer Programming? Nahh I'm a noob. I know the physical parts inside and out, but I'm here to learn the software side which I've never been taught.
